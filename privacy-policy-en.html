Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Voxory ("the App") is an offline voice-memo app that records audio and uses on-device AI to transcribe, summarize and categorize it. This policy explains what information the App processes and whether it leaves your device.

2. Information processed by the App

2-1. Processed only on your device (never transmitted)

The following stays only on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party:

  • Recorded audio (WAV) files
  • Transcribed text (STT results)
  • AI-generated titles, categories, keywords, summaries, emotions, to-dos
  • Audio quality metrics, category settings, app preferences

Stored in a local on-device database and app-private storage. You can delete it individually or entirely in the app; uninstalling the App removes it.

2-2. When network access occurs

The App connects to the internet only to:

  • Download AI models from external model hosts (e.g., Hugging Face / Google). Those hosts may log standard technical data (IP address, request time). We do not transmit your audio or text during this process.
  • Show ads (see Section 3).

3. Advertising & third-party data (Google AdMob)

The App serves ads via Google AdMob. AdMob and Google advertising partners may collect and share: Advertising ID, device information and identifiers, coarse location, and app usage/diagnostics data, in accordance with Google's policies.

  • Google Ads & Privacy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

You can reset your Advertising ID or limit ad personalization in device settings.

4. Permissions

5. Data retention & deletion

  • On-device data is kept until you delete it.
  • The App lets you delete individual memos, all memos, and recording files.
  • Uninstalling removes all App data on the device.
  • Ad-related data retention follows Google's policies.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under the applicable minimum age and does not knowingly collect their personal data.

7. Your rights

You may view, edit and delete on-device data at any time, and control ad personalization via device settings. For other requests, contact us below.
